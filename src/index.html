About Me

Hi! I'm Peter Hollmer, an engineering leader with 15+ years of experience in software who lives to set the stage for people to do their best work. I most recently served as Director of Engineering at SnapStream, where I had the privilege of leading 14 engineers across multiple teams and contributing to leadership decisions for a $10M ARR video software company.

My Leadership Philosophy

What most excites me is transforming engineering organizations through people-first leadership. I've learned that the best technical solutions come from teams that trust each other and feel empowered to innovate. Whether I was rebuilding team morale after downsizing or transforming a department's processes, I've found that creating a culture where quality matters and everyone's voice is heard makes all the difference.

Technical Background

My technical foundation was built through hands-on experience as an individual contributor at companies like Sony Creative Software, CS Disco, and Swimlane, with my expertise in cloud computing, AI integration, distributed systems, and mobile technologies developing during my leadership tenure at SnapStream.

This progression from individual contributor to engineering leader has given me deep appreciation for what engineers need to succeed and informs my people-first leadership approach, which centers on collaborative problem-solving and shared ownership.

What Drives Me

I'm passionate about mentoring engineering talent because I believe great software comes from great teams, not just great code. Right now, I'm excited about exploring new opportunities where I can leverage emerging technologies like cloud-native architectures and AI while continuing to build the kind of engineering organizations where people genuinely enjoy coming to work.
